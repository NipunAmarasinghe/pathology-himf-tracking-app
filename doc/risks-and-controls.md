# Risks and Controls

## Risks
- Incorrect expiry date entry
- Duplicate items due to inconsistent naming
- Permission misconfiguration leading to data integrity issues
- Barcode not recognised causing manual entry errors

## Controls
- Required fields for critical traceability fields (HIMF, Lot, Expiry)
- Dropdown standardisation for Category and Supplier
- Role-based permissions in SharePoint
- Validation and prompts for incorrect formats
